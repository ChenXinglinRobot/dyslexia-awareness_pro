# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** General public — people who have heard "阅读障碍" but cannot picture what it actually means. Parents, educators, students, curious strangers who arrive via search, word of mouth, or a shared link.

**Secondary:** Educators and parents actively seeking to understand dyslexia in children — motivated by personal stakes, looking for both emotional resonance and actionable understanding.

**Not the audience:** 阅读障碍儿童 themselves — the site is designed for neurotypical adults to build awareness and empathy.

## Product Purpose

「字向每人」 is an immersive awareness experience whose job is to make someone who has never encountered dyslexia genuinely *feel* the disorientation — and care enough to share it, talk about it, or act on it. The metric is emotional penetration: does the visitor leave with a visceral sense of what the experience is like, and does that change how they talk or think about dyslexia?

The experience must work on someone who has never heard the term, and must make them want to send the link to one specific person in their life. Every design and content decision serves that goal.

## Brand Personality

**Three-word personality: immersive, warm, authoritative**

The voice is restrained and powerful — like a documentary narrator who trusts the audience to receive difficult information without melodrama. Not preachy, not clinical. The brand speaks with quiet confidence: it has something real to show you, and it trusts you to receive it.

Example copy:
- 「一道乘法题，归零了整个世界」
- 「文字在纸上跳舞，他却只能看见碎片」
- 「亲手调一调，感受文字的呼吸」

Forbidden copy: generic welcomes ("欢迎来到我们的网站"), CTA clichés ("点击了解更多"), marketing buzzwords (empower, streamline, cutting-edge, next-generation, game-changer, mission-critical).

## Anti-references

**Specific patterns to avoid:**
- SaaS landing page scaffolding: hero → features → pricing → CTA
- Gradient text as a design element
- Glassmorphism or frosted glass decorative effects
- Numbered eyebrow sections (01 · About / 02 · Process)
- Tiny all-caps tracked kickers above every section heading
- Identical card grids (icon + heading + body repeated)
- Hero metric templates (big number + small label + gradient accent)
- Side-stripe borders as accent elements
- Uniform cream/sand/parchment body background (AI default warm neutral)
- The 55–95% AI-generated landing page scaffold: eyebrow + heading + body + CTA on every section

**Sites and patterns to explicitly NOT resemble:**
- Generic charity/disability awareness sites with stock photography and inspirational quotes over warm-toned backgrounds
- Clinical medical reference sites (WebMD-style): cold, clinical, data-first
- The "warm educational magazine" aesthetic — clean but forgettable, no emotional stakes

## Design Principles

1. **文字即主角** — Chinese character forms (strokes, radicals, structure) are the primary visual medium throughout the site. The design makes written language a physical, manipulable substance — fragments, particles, blur, clarity transitions. Visitors should feel they are looking *at language itself*, not at a page about language.

2. **情感冲击优先于信息完整** — A visitor who feels one thing deeply is worth more than a visitor who understands everything intellectually. Sections are designed as emotional moments first. Information is embedded in the experience, not presented as a reference document. There is no FAQ section, no glossary, no bullet-pointed facts unless they are dramatic enough to earn their place.

3. **克制中的温度** — The overall frame is austere and dark (deep ink blue). Warmth arrives only through amber light — a single signature accent color used sparingly. The cold structural frame makes the warm moments land harder. No decorative warmth; warmth only where it matters.

4. **交互即叙事** — Every interactive element (scroll, slider, toggle, click) advances the story from confusion to understanding. Interactions are not features; they are plot points. A slider controlling text blur is not an accessibility feature — it is the climax of the experience.

5. **分享摩擦最小化** — The site works as a direct URL. OG tags, meta descriptions, and thumbnail are set for social sharing, but the primary assumption is direct arrival. Shareability comes from the emotional quality of the experience, not from injected share buttons or interstitial referral prompts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

**Note:** This site is NOT designed to be readable by people with dyslexia. It is designed for neurotypical adults to build awareness and empathy. Simulation features (blur, jitter, overlap) are opt-in through explicit user controls — never automatic.

**For the general audience:**
- Color contrast: body text on dark background minimum WCAG AA (4.5:1); large text minimum 3:1
- Full keyboard navigation and focus management on all interactive elements
- Semantic HTML throughout, proper heading hierarchy, ARIA labels on interactive controls
- No information conveyed by color alone
- Text sizing and spacing optimized for general adult audience — readable without being overly spread out (line-height 1.8, not extreme letter-spacing)
